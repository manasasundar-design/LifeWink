Our story

LifeWink carries forth the greatest gifts we have in life – to love and to be loved

Hear from our Founder, Jim Baumann

Get started How it works

Over the past two decades, I've lost my parents and many cherished family members and friends. Those experiences shaped me deeply and reinforced something I believe to be true: to love and to be loved are life's greatest gift.

LifeWink was created from that belief.

LifeWink is a profoundly personal platform designed to help people authentically capture their love while they are living and share it with the people they love, today and beyond their lifetime. Our hope is that LifeWink inspires new conversations, deeper reflection, and more intentional connection, helping people express love today and carry it forward into the future.

Guided by love, integrity, curiosity, and excellence, we put our users' well-being at the center of everything we do. We are deeply committed to privacy, security, and ensuring every LifeWink remains authentic and true to the person who created it.

I often wish I could be receiving LifeWinks today from those I've loved and lost. It's my sincere hope that LifeWink earns your trust, feels meaningful to you, and helps you share what matters most with the people you love—today and beyond your lifetime.
