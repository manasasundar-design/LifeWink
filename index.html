WHAT IS LIFEWINK

LifeWink provides two ways to capture and share your love

Classic LifeWinks and AI LifeWinks

All LifeWinks are authentically created by you, the Beloved, and delivered to those you love, your Recipients, on-time and privately beyond your lifetime.

Classic LifeWinks

Authentically capture your most treasured moments using video, photos, audio, and attachments, and share them on dated, undated, or custom occasions with those you love—today and beyond your lifetime.

AI LifeWinks

Authentically capture what you value most through meaningful prompts and questions across six key topic areas and share it as a chat and voice AI experience with those you love—today and beyond your lifetime.

Create Today and Share Beyond Your Lifetime

As a Beloved, you create authentic Classic and AI LifeWinks today, which are shared with your Recipients beyond your lifetime, supported by your Stewards and Guardians.

Safe, Secure and Private

Anyone 18+ can create, share, and receive LifeWinks. Individuals under 18 can receive LifeWinks with guardian consent. Every LifeWink is authentic—built only by you, securely stored, and delivered privately and on time, exactly as you intend.
